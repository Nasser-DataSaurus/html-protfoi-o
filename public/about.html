About Me

I specialize in data-driven decision-making through Power BI dashboards, Python automation, and workflow optimization. I’ve developed tools that forecast demand, calculate optimal order quantities, and track KPIs across hundreds of stores and SKUs — reducing stockouts and improving efficiency. My work often focuses on turning raw data into clear, actionable insights that improve performance at scale.

Beyond analytics, I excel in leading projects and guiding teams through new system implementations, process standardization, and continuous improvement initiatives. From deploying automation workflows to authoring SOPs and SLAs, I aim to make operations more sustainable, measurable, and effective.

I am passionate about using technology and data to solve real-world business problems. Whether it’s optimizing supply chain performance, automating routine tasks, or delivering insights through BI tools, I thrive on projects that bring measurable results and lasting improvements.
